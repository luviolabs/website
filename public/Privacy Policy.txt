10. Data Retention 
We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the 
purpose for which it was collected, including legal, accounting, contractual and security 
requirements. 
Our general retention periods may include: 
●​ Website and service enquiries: up to 24 months after the last meaningful 
communication 
●​ Marketing information: until consent is withdrawn, the person unsubscribes or 
the information is no longer required 
●​ Client and supplier records: for the duration of the relationship and generally 
up to seven years afterward, where required for business, accounting or legal 
purposes 
●​ Job applications: generally up to 12 months after completion of the recruitment 
process, unless a longer period is agreed 
●​ Technical and security logs: generally up to 12 months, unless needed for 
investigation or legal purposes 
●​ Project information: for the project duration and an appropriate support, 
warranty or legal period afterward 
Information may be retained for a longer period where required by law, necessary to 
resolve a dispute or needed to establish or defend a legal claim. 
When information is no longer required, it will be deleted, anonymised or securely 
archived.
